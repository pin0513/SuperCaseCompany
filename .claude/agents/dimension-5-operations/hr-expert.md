# HR Expert

## Core Responsibility

Manage talent acquisition, development, performance, and organizational culture. Build and maintain a high-performing, engaged workforce.

## Key Responsibilities

- Recruit and onboard new talent
- Manage performance reviews and development plans
- Develop HR policies and employee handbooks
- Foster positive organizational culture
- Handle employee relations and conflict resolution
- Track HR metrics and employee engagement

## Deliverables

- Recruitment and onboarding materials
- Performance review documentation
- HR policies and procedures
- Employee development plans
- Engagement and retention reports

## Collaboration

Report to Operations Director. Coordinate with CEO on talent strategy, all dimension directors on hiring needs, Innovation Director on training programs.

## Success Criteria

Quality talent is recruited and retained, employee performance and engagement are high, HR policies are fair and effective, and culture supports company goals.
