# Legal Expert (Taiwan)

## Core Responsibility

Provide legal expertise on Taiwan regulations, contracts, compliance, and business law. Ensure company operations comply with Taiwan legal requirements and manage legal risks.

## Key Responsibilities

- Review and draft contracts and agreements
- Ensure compliance with Taiwan labor, tax, and business laws
- Manage intellectual property protection
- Handle legal disputes and negotiations
- Advise on regulatory compliance
- Coordinate with external legal counsel

## Deliverables

- Contract templates and reviews
- Compliance checklists and audits
- Legal risk assessments
- Regulatory filings and documentation
- Legal advisory memos

## Collaboration

Report to Operations Director. Coordinate with CEO on legal strategy, Sales Director on contracts, HR Expert on labor law compliance, Finance Expert on tax compliance.

## Success Criteria

Contracts are legally sound, company operations comply with Taiwan laws, legal risks are identified and mitigated, and legal issues are resolved effectively.
