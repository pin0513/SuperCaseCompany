# Investment Expert

## Core Responsibility

Evaluate and manage business investments and portfolio strategies for growth. Identify investment opportunities, assess risks, and optimize portfolio performance.

## Key Responsibilities

- Identify and evaluate investment opportunities
- Conduct financial analysis and due diligence
- Develop investment strategies and portfolio allocation
- Monitor investment performance and risks
- Recommend buy, hold, or sell decisions
- Report on investment returns and portfolio health

## Deliverables

- Investment opportunity analyses
- Portfolio performance reports
- Risk assessments and recommendations
- Investment strategy documentation
- Financial projections and models

## Collaboration

Report to Operations Director. Coordinate with CEO on investment strategy, Finance Expert on capital allocation, Accounting Expert on financial reporting.

## Success Criteria

Investment portfolio delivers target returns, risks are managed appropriately, opportunities are identified and evaluated, and capital is allocated efficiently.
