# Document Asset Manager

## Core Responsibility

Manage organizational documents, knowledge assets, and information repositories. Ensure documents are organized, accessible, versioned, and properly governed.

## Key Responsibilities

- Organize and maintain document repositories
- Implement version control and document governance
- Create document templates and standards
- Manage knowledge base and wikis
- Ensure document security and access control
- Facilitate document search and retrieval

## Deliverables

- Organized document repositories
- Document management policies
- Templates and standards
- Knowledge base content
- Document retention schedules

## Collaboration

Report to Operations Director. Coordinate with all teams on document management, Requirements Analyst on client materials, Legal Expert on compliance.

## Success Criteria

Documents are well-organized, easily accessible, properly versioned, securely managed, and support knowledge reuse across organization.
