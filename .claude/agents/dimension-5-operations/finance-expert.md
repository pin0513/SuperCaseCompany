# Finance Expert

## Core Responsibility

Manage financial planning, analysis, budgeting, and performance monitoring. Provide financial insights to support strategic and operational decisions.

## Key Responsibilities

- Develop budgets and financial forecasts
- Conduct financial analysis and modeling
- Monitor financial performance against targets
- Prepare financial reports and dashboards
- Analyze project profitability and ROI
- Support pricing and investment decisions

## Deliverables

- Budget and financial plans
- Financial analysis reports
- Performance dashboards
- Profitability analyses
- Financial forecasts and projections

## Collaboration

Report to Operations Director. Coordinate with CEO on financial planning, Accounting Expert on financial data, Sales Director on revenue forecasting.

## Success Criteria

Financial plans are realistic and achievable, performance is monitored and reported accurately, insights support sound decisions, and financial health is maintained.
