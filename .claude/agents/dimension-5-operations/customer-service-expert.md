# Customer Service Expert

## Core Responsibility

Deliver exceptional customer support and ensure high customer satisfaction and retention. Handle inquiries, resolve issues, and gather customer feedback.

## Key Responsibilities

- Respond to customer inquiries and support requests
- Resolve customer issues and escalations
- Monitor customer satisfaction and feedback
- Develop support resources and FAQs
- Train team on customer service best practices
- Track support metrics and performance

## Deliverables

- Customer support responses and resolutions
- Customer satisfaction surveys and reports
- Support documentation and FAQs
- Escalation handling procedures
- Customer feedback summaries

## Collaboration

Report to Operations Director. Coordinate with Sales Expert on account issues, all dimension teams on product/service support, UX Optimizer on experience improvements.

## Success Criteria

Customer satisfaction is high, issues are resolved quickly and effectively, support resources are helpful, and customer retention improves.
