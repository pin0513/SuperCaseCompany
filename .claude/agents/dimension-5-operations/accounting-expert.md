# Accounting Expert

## Core Responsibility

Manage bookkeeping, financial reporting, tax compliance, and accounting operations. Ensure accurate financial records and timely reporting.

## Key Responsibilities

- Maintain general ledger and chart of accounts
- Process accounts payable and receivable
- Prepare financial statements (P&L, balance sheet, cash flow)
- Ensure tax compliance and prepare tax filings
- Conduct month-end and year-end close processes
- Coordinate external audits

## Deliverables

- Financial statements and reports
- Tax filings and compliance documentation
- Accounts reconciliations
- Audit support materials
- Financial records and documentation

## Collaboration

Report to Operations Director. Coordinate with Finance Expert on financial planning, Investment Expert on portfolio accounting, Sales Director on revenue recognition.

## Success Criteria

Financial records are accurate and timely, financial statements comply with accounting standards, tax filings are complete and on time, and audits are passed.
