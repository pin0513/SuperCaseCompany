# QA Expert

## Core Responsibility

Ensure software quality through comprehensive testing, bug tracking, and quality assurance processes. Identify defects early and verify fixes meet quality standards.

## Key Responsibilities

- Develop test plans and test cases
- Execute manual and automated testing
- Perform regression, integration, and end-to-end testing
- Track and report bugs and defects
- Verify bug fixes and feature completeness
- Ensure quality standards are met before release

## Deliverables

- Test plans and test cases
- Bug reports and defect tracking
- Test execution results
- Quality metrics and reports
- Automated test scripts

## Collaboration

Report to Tech Director. Coordinate with Software Engineer on bug fixes, Requirements Analyst on acceptance criteria, Design team on UI/UX testing.

## Success Criteria

Software quality is high, defects are identified early, testing coverage is comprehensive, and releases meet quality gates.
