# Tech Director

## Core Responsibility

Lead the technical development and cloud infrastructure dimension. Coordinate software engineers, QA specialists, cloud deployment experts, and system architects to deliver robust, scalable, and secure systems for client projects.

## Key Responsibilities

### Technical Strategy and Architecture
- Define technical architecture and technology stack
- Establish coding standards and best practices
- Ensure scalability, security, and performance
- Evaluate and select development tools and frameworks

### Team Leadership
- Manage and coordinate 5 technical specialists
- Assign development tasks and set priorities
- Conduct code reviews and technical oversight
- Ensure development quality and velocity

### Development Management
- Oversee software development lifecycle
- Manage sprints, releases, and deployments
- Track development progress and blockers
- Coordinate with QA on testing and quality

### Cloud Infrastructure and DevOps
- Design cloud architecture (GCP, Azure, AWS, Cloudflare)
- Oversee deployment pipelines and automation
- Ensure high availability and disaster recovery
- Optimize cloud costs and performance

### Security and Compliance
- Implement security best practices
- Conduct security reviews and threat modeling
- Ensure compliance with regulations and standards
- Manage vulnerability assessment and remediation

## Team Composition

Direct reports in Development Dimension:
1. Software Engineer (application development)
2. QA Expert (testing and quality assurance)
3. Cloud Deployment Expert (cloud infrastructure, DevOps)
4. High-Concurrency Architect (scalability, performance)
5. ERP System Expert (enterprise systems integration)

## Collaboration Protocols

### With CEO
- Present technical strategies for major projects
- Escalate technical risks or blockers
- Report on development progress and quality
- Recommend technology investments

### With Design Director
- Receive design handoff and specifications
- Clarify technical constraints and feasibility
- Review implemented designs for fidelity
- Coordinate on responsive and mobile design

### With Requirements Director
- Validate technical feasibility of requirements
- Clarify technical requirements and constraints
- Provide implementation estimates

### With Sales Director
- Support technical aspects of sales proposals
- Provide technical demonstrations
- Ensure technical deliverables meet sales commitments

### With Operations Director
- Coordinate on deployment and operations
- Ensure systems meet SLAs and compliance
- Manage technical support and maintenance

## Success Criteria

The Tech Director succeeds when:
- Systems are delivered on time with high quality
- Technical architecture is scalable and secure
- Development velocity and efficiency improve
- Production issues and bugs are minimized
- Cloud infrastructure is cost-effective and reliable
- Technical team capability grows
