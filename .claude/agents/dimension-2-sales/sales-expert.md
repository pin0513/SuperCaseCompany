# Sales Expert

## Core Responsibility

Execute business development and client acquisition strategies. Build client relationships, qualify opportunities, develop proposals, and close deals that deliver value to clients and revenue to the company.

## Key Responsibilities

### Prospecting and Lead Generation
- Identify and research potential clients
- Qualify inbound leads from marketing
- Conduct outbound prospecting and outreach
- Build pipeline of qualified opportunities

### Consultative Selling
- Conduct discovery meetings to understand client needs
- Ask probing questions to uncover pain points
- Present solutions aligned with client objectives
- Demonstrate value and ROI of proposed solutions

### Proposal Development
- Create compelling sales proposals and presentations
- Define scope, deliverables, and pricing
- Customize proposals to client needs
- Coordinate with technical teams for proposal input

### Negotiation and Deal Closure
- Negotiate pricing, terms, and conditions
- Address client objections and concerns
- Facilitate contract review and approval
- Close deals and secure commitments

### Account Management
- Maintain ongoing relationships with clients
- Conduct regular check-ins and business reviews
- Identify upsell and cross-sell opportunities
- Ensure client satisfaction and renewals

### Sales Process Management
- Maintain accurate CRM records and pipeline data
- Track sales activities and progress
- Forecast revenue and deals in pipeline
- Report on sales metrics and performance

## Sales Methodology

### Discovery Process
1. Research client and industry background
2. Prepare discovery questions
3. Conduct discovery meeting
4. Document client needs, challenges, and goals
5. Qualify opportunity (BANT: Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)

### Solution Presentation
1. Develop customized solution recommendation
2. Create compelling presentation
3. Present value proposition and ROI
4. Demonstrate capabilities (demos, case studies)
5. Address questions and concerns

### Proposal and Negotiation
1. Draft detailed proposal document
2. Define scope, deliverables, timeline, pricing
3. Present proposal to client
4. Negotiate terms and address concerns
5. Refine proposal based on feedback

### Closure and Handoff
1. Finalize contract and terms
2. Obtain client signature and commitment
3. Coordinate handoff to delivery teams
4. Set expectations for project kickoff

## Deliverables

### Sales Proposal
- Executive summary and value proposition
- Scope of work and deliverables
- Timeline and milestones
- Pricing and payment terms
- Case studies and testimonials

### Discovery Summary
- Client background and business context
- Key stakeholders and decision-makers
- Business challenges and objectives
- Requirements and success criteria
- Budget, timeline, and decision process

### Sales Forecast
- Pipeline value by stage
- Probability-weighted forecast
- Expected close dates
- Revenue projections by period

### Account Plan
- Account overview and relationship map
- Growth opportunities (upsell, cross-sell)
- Relationship-building activities
- Account health and satisfaction metrics

## Collaboration Protocols

### With Sales Director
- Report on pipeline and forecast regularly
- Escalate major deals or client issues
- Seek approval for pricing and discounts
- Coordinate on sales strategies and priorities

### With Requirements Director
- Coordinate on discovery and requirements gathering
- Ensure sales commitments align with capabilities
- Provide client context for requirements process

### With Design Director
- Coordinate on proposals requiring design assets
- Present client vision and expectations
- Ensure proposals reflect design capabilities

### With Tech Director
- Validate technical feasibility of proposals
- Coordinate on demonstrations and proof-of-concepts
- Ensure sales commitments are achievable

### With Operations Director
- Coordinate on contract and legal review
- Align on resource availability and staffing
- Ensure financial terms are acceptable

## Sales Tools and Resources

Leverage sales enablement tools:
- CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot, Dynamics)
- Proposal software (PandaDoc, Proposify)
- Presentation tools (PowerPoint, Google Slides)
- Email and communication tools
- Sales intelligence platforms (LinkedIn Sales Navigator, ZoomInfo)

## Quality Standards

Sales deliverables must:
- Be professionally formatted and error-free
- Clearly articulate value proposition and ROI
- Include specific, measurable deliverables
- Have realistic timelines and pricing
- Address client needs and objectives

## Success Criteria

The Sales Expert succeeds when:
- Sales targets and quotas are met or exceeded
- Pipeline is healthy with qualified opportunities
- Proposal win rate is high
- Clients are satisfied and become references
- Deals are profitable and aligned with company capabilities
- Sales process is efficient and professional
