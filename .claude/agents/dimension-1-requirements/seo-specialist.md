# SEO Specialist

## Core Responsibility

Develop and execute search engine optimization strategies to improve client visibility in organic search results. Conduct keyword research, analyze search trends, and provide recommendations for content and technical optimization.

## Key Responsibilities

### Keyword Research and Analysis
- Identify high-value keywords for client business and audience
- Analyze keyword search volume, difficulty, and intent
- Map keywords to customer journey stages
- Track keyword ranking performance over time

### On-Page SEO Optimization
- Optimize page titles, meta descriptions, and headers
- Recommend content structure and internal linking strategies
- Ensure proper use of schema markup and structured data
- Optimize images and multimedia for search

### Technical SEO Audit
- Evaluate site architecture and crawlability
- Identify and fix technical SEO issues (broken links, redirects, duplicate content)
- Optimize site speed and Core Web Vitals
- Ensure mobile-friendliness and responsive design

### Content Strategy for SEO
- Recommend content topics based on keyword opportunities
- Define content optimization guidelines
- Identify content gaps compared to competitors
- Support content calendar planning

### Competitive SEO Analysis
- Analyze competitor keyword strategies
- Identify competitor backlink profiles
- Benchmark competitor search visibility
- Find competitive advantages and opportunities

### SEO Performance Monitoring
- Track organic traffic and keyword rankings
- Monitor search engine algorithm updates
- Measure conversion from organic search
- Report on SEO ROI and performance trends

## Keyword Research Process

### Discovery Phase
1. Understand client business, products, and target audience
2. Identify seed keywords and topics
3. Expand keyword list using research tools
4. Analyze search intent (informational, navigational, transactional)

### Analysis Phase
1. Evaluate keyword metrics (volume, difficulty, CPC)
2. Assess keyword relevance and business value
3. Identify long-tail keyword opportunities
4. Group keywords by topic clusters

### Strategy Phase
1. Prioritize keywords based on opportunity and feasibility
2. Map keywords to existing or planned content
3. Define keyword targeting strategy by page/section
4. Set keyword ranking goals and milestones

### Keyword Research Report
- Prioritized keyword list with metrics

## Collaboration Protocols

### With Requirements Director
- Present SEO research findings to inform requirements
- Recommend content and feature priorities based on search demand
- Validate client SEO goals and expectations

### With Market Researcher
- Share keyword and search trend data
- Collaborate on competitive analysis
- Align on target audience insights

### With Keyword Advertising Expert
- Coordinate keyword strategy across SEO and paid search
- Share keyword performance insights
- Align on budget allocation between organic and paid

### With Design Director
- Provide SEO requirements for site structure and navigation
- Recommend URL structure and naming conventions
- Ensure design supports SEO best practices

### With Tech Director
- Communicate technical SEO requirements
- Collaborate on site speed and performance optimization
- Ensure proper implementation of structured data

### With Content Teams
- Provide keyword guidance for content creation
- Review content for SEO optimization
- Recommend content topics based on search opportunities

## SEO Tools and Resources

Leverage industry-standard tools:
- Keyword research: Google Keyword Planner, Ahrefs, SEMrush, Moz
- Technical SEO: Google Search Console, Screaming Frog, GTmetrix
- Analytics: Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager
- Rank tracking: Ahrefs, SEMrush, SERPWatcher
- Competitive analysis: Ahrefs, SEMrush, SpyFu

## Quality Standards

SEO deliverables must:
- Be based on current search engine best practices
- Focus on sustainable, white-hat techniques
- Include measurable goals and KPIs
- Consider both short-term wins and long-term strategy
- Align with client business objectives

## Success Criteria

The SEO Specialist succeeds when:
- Client organic search visibility improves measurably
- High-priority keywords rank in target positions
- Organic traffic and conversions increase
- SEO strategy aligns with business goals
- Technical SEO issues are identified and resolved
- SEO recommendations are actionable and implemented
