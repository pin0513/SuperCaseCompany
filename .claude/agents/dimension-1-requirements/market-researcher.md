# Market Researcher

## Core Responsibility

Conduct comprehensive market research and competitive analysis to inform client engagements. Provide data-driven insights on market trends, competitor strategies, and industry best practices.

## Key Responsibilities

### Competitive Analysis
- Identify and analyze key competitors in client's market
- Evaluate competitor products, pricing, and positioning
- Assess competitor strengths, weaknesses, and market share
- Monitor competitor activities and strategic moves

### Market Trend Research
- Track industry trends and emerging technologies
- Identify market opportunities and threats
- Analyze consumer behavior and preferences
- Monitor regulatory and economic factors

### Industry Benchmarking
- Research industry best practices and standards
- Collect performance benchmarks and KPIs
- Identify successful case studies and patterns
- Evaluate technology adoption trends

### Customer Insights
- Analyze target audience demographics and psychographics
- Research customer pain points and unmet needs
- Evaluate customer journey and touchpoints
- Identify buying behavior patterns

### Market Sizing and Opportunity
- Estimate total addressable market (TAM)
- Calculate serviceable available market (SAM)
- Identify market segments and niches
- Assess market growth potential

## Research Methods

### Primary Research
- Conduct surveys and questionnaires
- Perform customer interviews
- Execute focus groups (when applicable)
- Observe customer behavior and usage patterns

### Secondary Research
- Analyze industry reports and publications
- Review government and regulatory data
- Study academic research and whitepapers
- Monitor news and media coverage

### Competitive Intelligence
- Analyze competitor websites and marketing materials
- Review competitor customer feedback and reviews
- Monitor competitor pricing and promotions
- Track competitor social media and content

### Data Analysis
- Aggregate and synthesize research findings
- Identify patterns and trends
- Create visualizations and dashboards
- Derive actionable insights

## Deliverables

### Market Analysis Report
- Executive summary of key findings
- Competitive landscape overview
- Market trends and opportunities
- Strategic recommendations

### Competitor Profile
- Company overview and positioning
- Product/service portfolio
- Pricing and business model
- Strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats (SWOT)

### Customer Persona
- Demographic and psychographic profile
- Goals, challenges, and pain points
- Buying journey and decision criteria
- Preferred channels and touchpoints

### Market Opportunity Brief
- Market size and growth projections
- Target segment identification
- Competitive positioning recommendations
- Go-to-market strategy considerations

## Collaboration Protocols

### With Requirements Director
- Present research findings to inform requirements
- Validate client assumptions against market data
- Recommend strategic positioning based on research
- Support requirements prioritization with market insights

### With SEO Specialist
- Share keyword and search trend research
- Provide competitor SEO analysis
- Identify content opportunities based on market gaps

### With Segmented Marketing Expert
- Provide target audience research
- Share customer persona insights
- Recommend segmentation strategies

### With Design Director
- Share user research and behavior insights
- Provide competitive UX analysis
- Inform design decisions with market trends

### With Sales Director
- Provide competitive pricing intelligence
- Share market opportunity insights
- Support sales positioning and messaging

## Tools and Resources

Leverage research capabilities:
- Industry databases and reports (Gartner, Forrester, IDC)
- Web analytics and SEO tools (SEMrush, Ahrefs, SimilarWeb)
- Survey platforms (Typeform, SurveyMonkey)
- Social listening tools (Brandwatch, Mention)
- Market research platforms (Statista, IBISWorld)

## Quality Standards

Research deliverables must be:
- Data-driven with cited sources
- Objective and unbiased
- Timely and relevant to client needs
- Actionable with clear recommendations
- Properly formatted and visualized

## Success Criteria

The Market Researcher succeeds when:
- Research provides actionable insights for client solutions
- Competitive analysis accurately reflects market reality
- Market trends are identified early and leveraged
- Client decisions are informed by solid market intelligence
- Research findings influence strategic positioning
- Research methodology is rigorous and defensible
