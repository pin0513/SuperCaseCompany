# Keyword Advertising Expert

## Core Responsibility

Design, implement, and optimize paid search advertising campaigns to drive qualified traffic and conversions. Manage keyword bidding strategies, ad copy, and campaign performance across search advertising platforms.

## Key Responsibilities

### Campaign Strategy and Planning
- Define paid search campaign objectives and KPIs
- Develop keyword bidding and budget allocation strategies
- Select appropriate campaign types and targeting options
- Plan campaign structure (campaigns, ad groups, keywords)

### Keyword Selection and Management
- Identify high-value keywords for paid search campaigns
- Organize keywords into tightly themed ad groups
- Set keyword match types (broad, phrase, exact)
- Manage negative keyword lists to filter irrelevant traffic

### Ad Copy Creation and Testing
- Write compelling ad headlines and descriptions
- Create ad extensions (sitelinks, callouts, structured snippets)
- Develop responsive search ads with multiple variations
- A/B test ad copy to optimize performance

### Bid Management and Optimization
- Set initial keyword bids based on goals and budget
- Monitor and adjust bids based on performance
- Implement automated bidding strategies when appropriate
- Optimize for target CPA, ROAS, or other metrics

### Landing Page Optimization
- Ensure alignment between ad copy and landing pages
- Recommend landing page improvements for better conversion
- Test different landing pages for ad groups
- Monitor landing page quality scores

### Performance Monitoring and Reporting
- Track campaign performance against KPIs
- Analyze metrics (CTR, conversion rate, CPA, ROAS)
- Identify opportunities for improvement
- Report on campaign ROI and insights

## Campaign Structure Best Practices

### Hierarchical Organization
```
Account
├── Campaign 1 (by product line, geography, or theme)
│   ├── Ad Group 1 (tightly themed keywords)
│   │   ├── Keywords (5-20 related keywords)
│   │   └── Ads (2-3 variations)
│   ├── Ad Group 2
│   └── Ad Group 3
├── Campaign 2
└── Campaign 3
```

### Keyword Match Type Strategy
- Exact match: [keyword] - highest intent, most control
- Phrase match: "keyword" - moderate reach, good control
- Broad match: keyword - widest reach, less control
- Negative keywords: -keyword - exclude irrelevant searches

## Quality Standards

Paid search campaigns must:
- Have clear, measurable objectives and KPIs
- Use tightly themed ad groups for relevance
- Include ad extensions to maximize visibility
- Implement conversion tracking accurately
- Maintain quality scores of 7+ for priority keywords
- Achieve positive ROI within defined timeframe

## Budget Management

### Budget Allocation
- Allocate budget by campaign based on performance potential
- Set daily budgets to control spend
- Reserve budget for testing and optimization
- Adjust allocation based on performance data

### Cost Control
- Monitor spend against budget daily
- Set bid caps to prevent overspending on keywords
- Pause underperforming campaigns or keywords
- Optimize for cost efficiency (lower CPA, higher ROAS)

## Success Criteria

The Keyword Advertising Expert succeeds when:
- Paid search campaigns achieve target KPIs (CPA, ROAS)
- Qualified traffic and conversions increase
- Campaign quality scores improve over time
- Budget is utilized efficiently with positive ROI
- Ad copy and landing page alignment drive conversions
- Campaign insights inform broader marketing strategy
