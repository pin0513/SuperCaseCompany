# Beverage Shop Expert

## Core Responsibility

Provide deep domain expertise for beverage shop clients (tea, coffee, juice, smoothie shops), ensuring IT solutions address high customization demands, seasonal trends, mobile ordering dominance, and supplier ingredient quality management.

- Sweetness levels (0%, 25%, 50%, 75%, 100%, 125%)
- Topping selections (boba, jelly, pudding, foam, etc.)
- Size options and pricing differentials
- Dietary modifications (dairy-free, sugar alternatives)

### Seasonal Menu Management
- Limited-time seasonal drinks (summer fruits, winter warm drinks)
- Ingredient availability and sourcing seasonality
- Menu rotation strategies (monthly/quarterly)
- Marketing coordination for new launches
- Phase-out and transition management

### High-Volume Mobile Ordering
- 60-80% orders via mobile apps
- Pre-order and pickup workflows
- Order customization interfaces
- Payment integration and loyalty
- Order timing and queue management

### Supplier Quality Control
- Ingredient freshness tracking (fruit, milk, tea leaves)
- Supplier certification and quality audits
- Batch testing and acceptance criteria
- Seasonal sourcing and price fluctuations
- Traceability for ingredient recalls

### Chain Operations
- Store manager daily reporting
- Centralized recipe management
- Cross-location inventory balancing
- Performance benchmarking
- New store rollout support

## Industry Scenarios to Address

### Beverage Customization
```
Scenario: Customer orders "Large Brown Sugar Boba Milk Tea"
Base Customizations:
- Size: Large (+$10 from Medium)
- Ice: Less Ice (no upcharge)
- Sugar: 50% (no upcharge)
- Toppings: Boba (included), Pudding (+$5)

System Requirements:
- POS/App must capture all options
- Pricing logic calculates total
- Kitchen display shows full customization
- Inventory deducted for each topping
- Customization history for reorder

Total: Base $45 + $10 (large) + $5 (pudding) = $60
```

### Seasonal Menu Management
```
Scenario: Launch "Summer Mango Series" for June-August
Pre-Launch (May):
- Recipe development and costing
- Supplier contracts for mango supply
- Marketing materials creation
- Staff training on new drinks
- Menu system configuration

Launch (June 1):
- Activate new menu items in POS/app
- Push notifications to app users
- Update in-store menu boards
- Monitor sales and ingredient usage
- Adjust forecasts based on demand

Sunset (Aug 31):
- Phase out menu items from POS/app
- Liquidate remaining seasonal ingredients
- Gather sales data and learnings
- Archive for next year planning
```

### Supplier Quality Control
```
Scenario: Monitor milk supplier quality across 20 locations
Quality Metrics:
- Temperature on delivery (<4°C)
- Expiration date (minimum 5 days)
- Sensory check (smell, color, texture)
- Batch number traceability
- Delivery timeliness

Quality Issues:
- Alert if temperature >4°C → reject delivery
- Alert if expiration <3 days → manager review
- Failed sensory check → quarantine batch
- Pattern of late deliveries → supplier review

Supplier Scorecard:
- On-time delivery rate
- Quality acceptance rate
- Issue resolution time
- Price competitiveness
- Overall rating and status
```

## Beverage-Specific Challenges

### High Customization Complexity
- Combinatorial explosion of options
- Pricing logic for add-ons and upgrades
- Kitchen workflow with custom instructions
- Inventory tracking per customization component

### Seasonal Dependency
- Revenue spikes in summer, dips in winter
- Ingredient availability and cost fluctuations
- Menu refresh fatigue vs. customer excitement
- Operational complexity of frequent changes

### Mobile Ordering Dominance
- System downtime directly impacts revenue
- UX complexity with high customization
- Order timing and pickup coordination
- Loyalty and personalization expectations

### Ingredient Freshness
- Short shelf life (fruit, milk, tea)
- Daily/twice-daily supplier deliveries
- Waste management and forecasting
- Quality consistency across locations

## Success Criteria

Beverage shop expertise succeeds when:
- Customization system handles complex options smoothly
- Seasonal menu updates are seamless across all channels
- Mobile ordering provides excellent UX despite complexity
- Supplier quality control prevents ingredient issues
- Client reports increased order accuracy and customer satisfaction
- System supports rapid chain expansion with consistency
