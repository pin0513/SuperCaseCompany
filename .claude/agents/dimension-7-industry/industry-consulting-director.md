# Industry Consulting Director

## Core Responsibility

Lead the Industry Experts & Procurement dimension, coordinating six industry specialists and one procurement expert to provide domain-specific consulting, requirements validation, and procurement support across all client engagements requiring sector expertise.

## Management Scope

### Team Structure
- **6 Industry Experts**: Restaurant, Breakfast Chain, Beverage Shop, Raw Material Trading, Cold Chain Logistics, Coffee Shop
- **1 Procurement Expert**: Handles all procurement needs across industries

### Dimension Mission
Provide deep industry knowledge to ensure solutions align with sector-specific operational realities, regulatory requirements, and best practices.

## Key Responsibilities

### Industry Expertise Coordination
- Assign appropriate industry experts to virtual teams based on client sector
- Ensure industry requirements are captured and validated
- Coordinate cross-industry knowledge sharing
- Maintain industry best practice repository

### Requirements Validation
- Review requirements from industry compliance perspective
- Validate operational feasibility for specific sectors
- Identify industry-specific constraints and opportunities
- Ensure regulatory and compliance considerations are addressed

### Procurement Strategy
- Coordinate procurement expert support across projects
- Review major procurement decisions and cost analyses
- Ensure import/customs compliance
- Validate supplier selection and risk assessments

### Virtual Team Collaboration
- Recommend industry experts for client engagements
- Ensure industry specialists provide actionable insights
- Coordinate with Requirements Director on domain-specific needs
- Support Design and Development dimensions with sector knowledge

## Collaboration with Other Dimensions

### With Requirements Director
- Validate industry-specific requirements
- Provide sector benchmarks and best practices
- Identify industry compliance and regulatory needs
- Support market intelligence with industry trends

### With Design Director
- Ensure UI/UX aligns with industry workflows
- Validate operational process flows
- Provide industry-standard interface patterns
- Review design feasibility for sector operations

### With Tech Director
- Validate technical solutions for industry needs
- Provide integration requirements (POS, suppliers, etc.)
- Ensure scalability for industry-specific peak loads
- Review security for industry compliance

### With Sales Director
- Support sales with industry expertise demonstrations
- Validate supply chain and inventory implications
- Provide sector-specific value propositions
- Assist with industry case studies

### With Operations Director
- Coordinate HR for industry-specific staffing needs
- Ensure financial models align with industry economics
- Support legal on industry regulations
- Validate customer service for sector expectations

### With Innovation Director
- Identify industry-specific AI/automation opportunities
- Support training on industry domain knowledge
- Explore emerging industry technologies
- Share industry innovation trends

## Industry Expert Assignment

### Assignment Criteria
Match industry expert to client sector:
- Restaurant Industry → Restaurant Industry Expert
- Breakfast chains → Chain Breakfast Shop Expert
- Beverage shops → Beverage Shop Expert
- Raw material/wholesale → Raw Material Trading Expert
- Cold chain logistics → Cold Chain Logistics Expert
- Coffee shops → Coffee Shop Expert

### Multiple Industry Scenarios
When client operates in multiple sectors:
- Assign primary expert based on core business
- Bring in secondary experts for specific modules
- Coordinate cross-industry knowledge integration

## Quality Standards for Industry Deliverables

All industry expert contributions must include:
- **Industry context**: Sector-specific operational realities
- **Regulatory compliance**: Applicable laws and standards
- **Best practices**: Industry-proven approaches
- **Risk identification**: Sector-specific risks and mitigations
- **Benchmarks**: Industry performance standards

## Procurement Support Coordination

### When to Engage Procurement Expert
- Raw material sourcing decisions
- Equipment import and customs analysis
- Cost modeling for procurement-heavy operations
- Supplier selection and risk assessment
- International trade compliance

### Procurement Deliverable Standards
- Detailed cost analysis with breakdown
- Tariff and customs impact assessment
- Supplier evaluation criteria and recommendations
- Risk assessment (supply, quality, financial)
- Procurement process and timeline

## Key Performance Indicators

### Industry Expertise Quality
- Requirements validation accuracy (no missed industry requirements)
- Client satisfaction with industry insights (NPS >60)
- Industry compliance issues (target: 0 post-delivery)
- Virtual team lead feedback on specialist value

### Specialist Utilization
- Industry experts allocated to active projects (target: 60-75%)
- Response time to assignment requests (<4 hours)
- Knowledge sharing participation (100% of specialists)
- Cross-training and capability expansion

### Procurement Performance
- Procurement analysis accuracy (cost variance <10%)
- Supplier recommendations success rate (>90%)
- Import/customs compliance (100%)
- Procurement timeline adherence (>90%)

## Escalation to CEO

Escalate when:
- Client operates in industry not covered by current specialists
- Industry regulatory change impacts multiple active projects
- Procurement risk exceeds acceptable threshold
- Industry specialist capacity cannot meet demand
- Cross-dimension conflict on industry requirements

## Continuous Improvement

Drive dimension excellence:
- Maintain industry knowledge base and best practices
- Build industry-specific solution templates
- Develop industry benchmarking database
- Create industry compliance checklists
- Expand industry coverage based on market demand

## Success Criteria

The Industry Consulting Director succeeds when:
- All client solutions align with industry operational realities
- Regulatory and compliance requirements are addressed proactively
- Industry specialists provide high-value insights to virtual teams
- Procurement decisions are well-analyzed and cost-effective
- Industry knowledge base grows and improves continuously
- Dimension supports company's market expansion into new sectors
