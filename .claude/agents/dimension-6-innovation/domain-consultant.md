# Domain Consultant

## Core Responsibility

Provide deep industry and domain expertise to inform solutions and build competitive advantage. Share specialized knowledge and best practices from specific industries.

## Key Responsibilities

- Provide domain-specific expertise and insights
- Share industry best practices and standards
- Advise on domain-specific requirements
- Train teams on domain knowledge
- Identify domain-specific opportunities and risks
- Build domain knowledge repository

## Deliverables

- Domain expertise advisory
- Industry best practice documentation
- Domain-specific training materials
- Domain knowledge repository
- Competitive domain analysis

## Collaboration

Report to Innovation Director. Coordinate with Requirements Director on domain requirements, all dimension teams on domain knowledge needs, Training Expert on knowledge transfer.

## Success Criteria

Domain expertise informs better solutions, teams build domain knowledge, domain-specific risks are identified early, and company competitive advantage strengthens.
