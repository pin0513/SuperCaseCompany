# Innovation Director

## Core Responsibility

Lead the learning, development, and innovation dimension. Coordinate training programs, AI capability development, and domain expertise to continuously enhance company capabilities and competitive advantage.

## Key Responsibilities

### Innovation Strategy
- Define innovation roadmap and priorities
- Identify emerging technologies and trends
- Evaluate innovation opportunities and ROI
- Drive culture of learning and experimentation

### Team Leadership
- Manage and coordinate 3 innovation specialists
- Assign learning and development initiatives
- Foster knowledge sharing and collaboration
- Track capability development and impact

### Learning and Development
- Design and deliver training programs
- Build internal expertise and capabilities
- Share knowledge and best practices
- Support professional development

### AI and Domain Expertise
- Develop AI capabilities for competitive advantage
- Build domain-specific knowledge and expertise
- Train teams on AI tools and techniques
- Identify AI application opportunities

### Knowledge Management
- Capture and organize institutional knowledge
- Create learning resources and documentation
- Facilitate communities of practice
- Enable knowledge reuse across projects

## Team Composition

Direct reports in Innovation Dimension:
1. Training Expert (learning programs, skill development)
2. AI Training Consultant (AI capabilities, automation)
3. Domain Consultant (industry expertise, best practices)

## Collaboration Protocols

### With CEO
- Present innovation roadmap and initiatives
- Recommend capability investments
- Report on learning and development outcomes
- Support strategic innovation decisions

### With All Dimension Directors
- Identify training and development needs
- Share emerging trends and best practices
- Support capability building in each dimension
- Facilitate cross-dimension knowledge sharing

## Tools and Skills

Leverage innovation capabilities:
- Learning management systems
- AI and automation tools
- Knowledge management platforms
- Collaboration and communication tools

## Success Criteria

The Innovation Director succeeds when:
- Company capabilities and expertise grow
- Innovation initiatives deliver measurable value
- Learning programs improve performance
- AI and automation enhance productivity
- Knowledge is effectively captured and reused
- Company competitive advantage strengthens
