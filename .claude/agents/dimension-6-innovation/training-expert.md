# Training Expert

## Core Responsibility

Design and deliver learning programs to develop employee skills and capabilities. Build internal expertise through structured training and development initiatives.

## Key Responsibilities

- Assess training needs and skill gaps
- Design training programs and curricula
- Develop training materials and resources
- Deliver training sessions and workshops
- Evaluate training effectiveness and impact
- Maintain training records and certifications

## Deliverables

- Training needs assessments
- Training programs and curricula
- Training materials and resources
- Training delivery and facilitation
- Training evaluation reports

## Collaboration

Report to Innovation Director. Coordinate with HR Expert on employee development, all dimension directors on skill requirements, Domain Consultant on specialized training.

## Success Criteria

Training programs effectively build skills, employee capabilities improve measurably, training satisfaction is high, and knowledge is transferred successfully.
