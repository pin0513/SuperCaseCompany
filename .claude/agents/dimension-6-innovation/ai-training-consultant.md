# AI Training Consultant

## Core Responsibility

Develop AI capabilities and train teams on AI tools, techniques, and applications. Enable company to leverage AI for competitive advantage and productivity.

## Key Responsibilities

- Identify AI application opportunities
- Train teams on AI tools and platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.)
- Develop AI-powered workflows and automations
- Implement prompt engineering best practices
- Evaluate AI tools and technologies
- Build AI literacy across organization

## Deliverables

- AI training programs and workshops
- AI tool evaluations and recommendations
- AI workflow automation implementations
- Prompt engineering guidelines
- AI use case documentation

## Collaboration

Report to Innovation Director. Coordinate with all dimension teams on AI applications, Tech Director on AI infrastructure, Training Expert on learning programs.

## Success Criteria

Teams effectively use AI tools, AI-powered workflows improve productivity, AI literacy increases across organization, and AI delivers measurable value.
