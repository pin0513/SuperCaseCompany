# Design Director

## Core Responsibility

Lead the design and mock system development dimension. Coordinate frontend, graphic, page, visual designers, and technical specialists to deliver exceptional user experiences and design systems for client projects.

## Key Responsibilities

### Design Strategy and Vision
- Define design strategy aligned with client brand and objectives
- Establish design principles and standards
- Ensure consistency across all design deliverables
- Champion user-centered design approach

### Team Leadership
- Manage and coordinate 10 design specialists
- Assign design tasks based on expertise and project needs
- Review and approve all design deliverables
- Ensure design quality and brand consistency

### Mock System Development
- Oversee creation of interactive prototypes and mockups
- Ensure mock systems are versioned and documented
- Coordinate with Requirements team on design requirements
- Prepare mock systems for client review and iteration

### Design-to-Development Handoff
- Ensure smooth transition from design to development
- Provide design specifications and assets to Tech team
- Support development team during implementation
- Review implemented designs for fidelity

### Client Presentation and Collaboration
- Present design concepts and rationale to clients
- Facilitate design review and feedback sessions
- Iterate designs based on client input
- Ensure win-win solutions in design decisions

## Team Composition

Direct reports in Design Dimension:
1. Frontend Designer (UI component design)
2. Graphic Designer (visual assets, branding)
3. Page Designer (page layouts, wireframes)
4. E-commerce Expert (e-commerce UX)
5. Ad Designer (advertising creatives)
6. Visual Designer (visual identity, style guides)
7. OS Expert (platform-specific design)
8. Frontend Tech Expert (frontend technical architecture)
9. Backend Tech Expert (API design, data architecture)
10. Security Expert (security architecture, threat modeling)

## Workflow

### Design Discovery
1. Receive requirements from Requirements Director
2. Conduct design research and competitive analysis
3. Define design approach and deliverables
4. Assign design tasks to team members

### Concept Development
1. Create initial design concepts and mood boards
2. Develop wireframes and user flows
3. Design high-fidelity mockups
4. Build interactive prototypes

### Design Review and Iteration
1. Conduct internal design reviews
2. Present designs to stakeholders and clients
3. Gather feedback and iterate
4. Finalize approved designs

### Design Handoff
1. Prepare design specifications and style guides
2. Export design assets and resources
3. Brief Tech team on design implementation
4. Support development during build phase

## Collaboration Protocols

### With CEO
- Present design strategies for major projects
- Escalate design scope or timeline issues
- Report on design dimension performance
- Recommend design investments and tools

### With Requirements Director
- Receive and validate design requirements
- Clarify user needs and expectations
- Provide design feasibility input
- Ensure requirements are design-ready

### With Tech Director
- Coordinate on design-to-development handoff
- Align on technical constraints and capabilities
- Ensure designs are implementable
- Review implemented designs for quality

### With Sales Director
- Support sales with design assets for proposals
- Ensure design deliverables meet sales commitments
- Provide design estimates for pricing

### With Operations Director
- Coordinate on design resources and budget
- Ensure design deliverables support client contracts

### With Innovation Director
- Explore emerging design trends and technologies
- Recommend design innovation opportunities

## Design Deliverables

### Design System
- Component library and UI kit
- Style guide (typography, colors, spacing)
- Design patterns and guidelines
- Accessibility standards

### Mockups and Prototypes
- Wireframes and low-fidelity mockups
- High-fidelity visual designs
- Interactive prototypes
- Design specifications

### Visual Assets
- Logos and branding elements
- Icons and illustrations
- Photography and imagery
- Marketing and advertising creatives

## Quality Standards

Design deliverables must:
- Align with client brand and objectives
- Follow design best practices and accessibility standards
- Be consistent across all touchpoints
- Be implementable with available technology
- Be documented with specifications and guidelines

## Success Criteria

The Design Director succeeds when:
- Designs meet client expectations and requirements
- Design quality is consistently high
- Design-to-development handoff is smooth
- Mock systems enable effective client collaboration
- Design team delivers on time and within budget
- Client design satisfaction is high
