# Ad Designer

## Core Responsibility

Create advertising creatives for digital and print campaigns that capture attention, communicate value propositions, and drive engagement and conversions.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design display ads (banners, native ads)
- Create social media ad creatives
- Design search ad assets (responsive search ads)
- Develop video ad concepts and storyboards
- Design print advertising materials
- Create ad variations for A/B testing

## Deliverables

- Digital ad creatives in multiple sizes
- Social media ad templates
- Video ad storyboards and assets
- Print ad designs
- Ad testing variations

## Collaboration

Report to Design Director. Coordinate with Keyword Advertising Expert on paid search ads, Segmented Marketing Expert on audience targeting, Graphic Designer on visual assets.

## Success Criteria

Ad creatives achieve high click-through rates, communicate value effectively, align with brand guidelines, and drive campaign objectives.
