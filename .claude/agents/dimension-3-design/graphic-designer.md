# Graphic Designer

## Core Responsibility

Create visual assets including logos, icons, illustrations, and branding elements that communicate brand identity and enhance visual communication.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design logos and brand identity systems
- Create icons and iconography sets
- Develop illustrations and graphics
- Design marketing collateral and print materials
- Ensure brand consistency across assets
- Prepare assets for digital and print production

## Deliverables

- Logo designs and brand guidelines
- Icon sets and illustration libraries
- Marketing graphics and templates
- Print-ready files and specifications
- Asset libraries and usage guidelines

## Collaboration

Report to Design Director. Coordinate with Visual Designer on brand identity, Ad Designer on marketing materials, Frontend Designer on digital assets.

## Success Criteria

Visual assets are on-brand, professionally crafted, versatile across media, and effectively communicate intended messages.
