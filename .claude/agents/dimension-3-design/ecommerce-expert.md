# E-commerce Expert

## Core Responsibility

Design e-commerce user experiences optimized for product discovery, conversion, and customer satisfaction. Apply e-commerce best practices to maximize sales and reduce friction.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design product listing and detail pages
- Optimize shopping cart and checkout flows
- Design search and filtering experiences
- Create product recommendation strategies
- Design account and order management interfaces
- Optimize for mobile commerce

## Deliverables

- E-commerce page designs (PLP, PDP, cart, checkout)
- Conversion optimization recommendations
- Mobile commerce designs
- Search and filtering specifications
- Personalization and recommendation strategies

## Collaboration

Report to Design Director. Coordinate with Page Designer on layouts, Frontend Designer on components, Sales Expert on conversion goals.

## Success Criteria

E-commerce designs drive product discovery, minimize cart abandonment, maximize conversion rates, and deliver satisfying shopping experiences.
